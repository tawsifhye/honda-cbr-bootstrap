Honda CBR 650R

Honda CBR 650R is a sports bike available at a starting price of Rs. 8,88,221 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The Honda CBR 650R is powered by 648.72cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 85.82 bhp and a torque of 57.5 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Honda CBR 650R comes up with anti-locking braking system. This CBR 650R bike weighs 211 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 15.4 liters. The Honda CBR650R has received mechanical upgrades to comply with the new, more stringent emission norms. The BS6 compliant model, which will rival the likes of Kawasaki Ninja 650 and the Ninja ZX-6R, carries a substantial price premium over its predecessor.
